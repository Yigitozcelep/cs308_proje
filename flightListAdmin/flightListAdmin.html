Welcome to Flights List Help

In the "Flights List" page, you can browse and filter available flights for administration purposes.

Filtering Flights

Use the input fields to filter flights by parameters in the table. Click the "APPLY" button to apply the filters.

Table Display

The table displays available flights with columns for Flight No, Plane ID, Departure Date, Departure Place, Departure Airport, Arrival Date, Arrival Place, Arrival Airport and Vehicle Type. You can click on the column headers to sort the table.

Flight Management

To manage flights, you can add a new flight or update an existing one by clicking on the respective buttons provided.

Flight Selection

To select a flight for further actions, such as editing and viewing detailed info, click on the "Select" button next to the corresponding row in the table.

Flight Deletion

To delete a flight, click on the "Delete" button next to the corresponding row in the table.

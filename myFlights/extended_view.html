Welcome to My Flights Help

In the "My Flights" page, you can view and manage your booked flights.

Filtering Flights

Use the input fields to filter flights by booking number, flight number, departure/arrival dates, places, seat type, and seat number. Click the "APPLY" button to apply the filters.

Table Display

The table displays your flights with columns for Booking No, Flight No, Departure Date, Departure Place, Arrival Date, Arrival Place, Seat Type, and Seat Number. You can click on the column headers to sort the table.

Deleting Flights

To delete a flight, click on the "Delete" button next to the corresponding row in the table. Deleted flights will be removed permanently.

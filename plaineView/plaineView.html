Welcome to Flights List Help

In the "Flights List" page, you can browse and filter available flights to book.

Filtering Flights

Use the input fields to filter flights by flight number, plane ID, departure/arrival dates, departure/arrival places, and vehicle type. Click the "APPLY" button to apply the filters.

Table Display

The table displays available flights with columns for Flight No, Plane ID, Departure Date, Departure Place, Arrival Date, Arrival Place, and Vehicle Type. You can click on the column headers to sort the table.

Booking Flights

To book a flight, click on the "Book" button next to the corresponding row in the table. You will be directed to the booking page for that flight.
